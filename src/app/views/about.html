About 5-Coy

5th Company - IV Field Regiment of the Colonial Marine Corps

Tasked by the United Worlds government to travel from star system to star system - dealing with dangers on different planets - with extreme predjudice.

From 1988 to 1996, a group of fun-loving LARP players came together in a unique science-ficion universe to act out the adventures of the 5th Company Colonial Marine Corps. Set in the year 2488 onwards, 5-coy battled corporations, genetically engineered soldiers, their own selves from a parallel dimension, and even aliens as they strived to protect the United Worlds.

Professionally run by first Event Horizons, and later its successor Dark Horizons, the games were unique at the time in providing a fully imersive experience - from Friday night to Sunday afternoon the players would be members of the Corps.

This is an archive of all the in and out of character information that exists still - mission reports, briefing packs, training materials, or whatever. Scanned at high resolution, we intend on using OCR to create searcheable HTML versions of the text - so we can share the stories that we created a quarter century ago in the 25th Century.
